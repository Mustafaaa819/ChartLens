01  How it works

Three steps from a banker's box to a working timeline.

Drop in the record exactly as you received it. ChartLens does the reading, the ordering, and the cross-checking — and shows its source for everything.

STEP 01

Upload the whole file

PDFs, scans, handwritten notes, EHR exports — thousands of pages at once. No tagging, no pre-sorting.

STEP 02

Get a date-ordered chronology

Every encounter on a single timeline: date, provider, facility, and treatment — each line annotated and linked to its source page.
